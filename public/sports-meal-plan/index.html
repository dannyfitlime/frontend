Who a sports meal plan is for

Recreational athletes

If you train regularly and want nutrition that supports your activity better, a structured plan can improve consistency and make eating around training easier.

Performance-focused sport

When performance matters, it becomes even more important to align food intake with workload and recovery.

Sport plus everyday life

Most people do not train in laboratory conditions. The plan has to work alongside a normal schedule and daily obligations.

Why adding more food is not enough

Sports nutrition is not just about eating more because you exercise. What matters is when, why, and in what context. Fat loss, maintenance, and a demanding training week all require different approaches. A good plan should support both sport and everyday life instead of creating more friction.
