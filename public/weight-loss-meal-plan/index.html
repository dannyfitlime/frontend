Why generic advice is not enough

Weight loss often fails because the advice is too general. One person needs a better meal distribution through the day, another needs more suitable portions, and someone else has to align eating with training or work shifts. Personalization matters because the goal is not just to eat less, but to eat in a way that is sensible and sustainable.
