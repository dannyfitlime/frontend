Cookies

We use cookies on FitLime so the site works correctly, stays secure, and we can keep improving it. Below is a summary of the cookie types, why we use them, and how to manage your consent.

How cookies work

Cookies are small text files saved in your browser. They let the site remember basic settings so everything runs smoothly and securely.

  • Keep language, form content, and other essential choices.
  • Help maintain secure operation and protect against abuse.
  • Optionally collect anonymized traffic data to improve the site.

Which types we use

  • Essential Power the site (language choice, form state). They cannot be turned off because the site would not work.
  • Analytics Anonymous measurement of traffic and performance. Enabled only after your consent.
  • Marketing Personalization and third-party campaigns. Used only if you explicitly allow them.

How to change or withdraw consent

  1. On the homepage, a banner appears on first visit so you can accept or decline optional cookies.
  2. You can reset your choice anytime via the button below or by clearing cookies in your browser.
  3. Technical cookies stay active so the site can run and show you the consent banner again.
